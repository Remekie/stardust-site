The Cocktail

The Golden Standard

After-hours drinks. VIP gifting. Elevated pours.

Ingredients

  • Zyra Gold 2 oz
  • Dry vermouth 0.75 oz
  • Orange bitters dash
  • Lemon twist to garnish

Method

Stir with ice until properly chilled. Strain into a coupe glass. Garnish with a lemon peel.

Our Story

It started as late-night flavour experiments.

Zyra began as a series of late-night flavour experiments among friends who thought vodka was a blank canvas for bold ideas. Three years later, one of those ideas is North America's only craft root beer vodka.

The vodka is distilled thirty times for exceptional smoothness, using carefully selected grains and small-batch methods to ensure consistency. The flavors are cold-infused, not artificially flavored. The difference is in what you don't taste.
