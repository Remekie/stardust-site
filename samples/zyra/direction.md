# Direction — Zyra Vodka Redesign

## Resolved direction

Reading "Anti-Vibe Design Mandate + YOLO mode":
- **register:** `brand` — e-commerce marketing site; design IS the product
- **expressive axis:** `committed` → `drenched` — physical reality of the product drives every token
- **tone:** `playful` but not giggly — confident, a little dangerous, earned
- **density:** `balanced` — brand register default; commercial-conversion priority
- **distinctiveness:** `singular` — hard constraint from mandate; every section passes specificity test
- **audience:** Design-aware, DTC-native buyer; Canadian/American spirits market; 25–42; Instagram-first
- **ia-fidelity:** `reimagined` — full IA rethink around buyer decision journey
- **constraints:** Anti-vibe full 8-tell checklist; no cream ground; dark ground required

## Font deck: retro-italian

- **Display:** Alfa Slab One — heavy slab serif, enamel-sign physicality, not on reflex-reject list
- **Body:** Hanken Grotesk — information-panel voice, pairs with enamel sign display type

**Reflex-reject fonts NOT used:** Inter, DM Sans, Outfit, Syne, Fraunces, Cormorant Garamond, Space Grotesk, Playfair Display

## IA — buyer decision journey

The current site's IA (generic hero → mood quiz → footer) replaced by the actual buyer's decision path:

1. **Am I in the right place?** — Hero: "North America's only craft root beer vodka." (specific, unarguable)
2. **What can I get?** — Three flavor lineup, editorially treated
3. **Why Root Beer Rush?** — The specific claim earns its own section
4. **What do I make with it?** — One cocktail. Not a quiz.
5. **Who made this?** — Origin, briefly
6. **Buy it** — Single CTA band

## Copy rules

- Every headline passes specificity test: could it belong to any other vodka brand?
- No marketing adjectives: artisanal, crafted, premium, curated, tropical (generic), bold (generic)
- One exclamation point maximum per page — earned, not reflexive
- Use what only Zyra can say: "North America's only craft root beer vodka", "30× distilled", "triticale base", "cold-infused with real sarsaparilla root"

## Variant theses

**A — Anti-vibe primary:** The full mandate executed. Enamel-sign dark, buyer journey IA, specificity-tested copy, editorial hairline grid, single CTA.

**B — The claim is the hero:** Lead with the unarguable sentence at display scale. Tests whether copy is a stronger hook than the bottle.

**C — Three vodkas, immediately:** All three SKUs above the fold. Commerce-first entry.
