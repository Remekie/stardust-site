Common questions

How car title loans work.

How do car title loans work?
A car title loan uses your vehicle as collateral. You keep driving — we hold the title, not the car. Approval is based on vehicle equity (market value minus balance owing). No credit check because the loan is secured by an asset you already own.
Can I get approved with bad credit in Edmonton or Calgary?
Yes. We do not check credit. We have approved applicants with bad credit, no credit history, previous bankruptcy, consumer proposals, and new immigrants with no Canadian credit file.
How much can I borrow against my vehicle?
$4,000 to $50,000 depending on current market value and any balance owing. Call (780) 995-1579 for an instant estimate — no paperwork for the quote.
Do I keep my car during the loan?
Yes. You keep driving throughout the entire term. We hold the title as collateral only — your car is never taken.
What are the rates and terms?
APR 9%–35% depending on vehicle value and term. Up to 3 years. No early payout penalty. Example: $5,600 over 12 months at 31.76% APR = $7,661.92 total. Full terms disclosed before you sign.
