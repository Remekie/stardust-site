Common questions

How car title loans work.

How do car title loans work?
A car title loan uses your vehicle as collateral. You keep driving while the loan is outstanding — we hold the title, not the car. Approval is based on your vehicle's equity (market value minus any balance owing). No credit check required because the loan is secured by an asset you already own.
Do I need good credit to qualify in Edmonton?
No. We do not run a credit check. Approval is based entirely on your vehicle's equity. We have approved applicants with bad credit, no credit history, previous bankruptcy, consumer proposals, and new immigrants with no Canadian credit file.
How much can I borrow against my vehicle?
Loan amounts range from $4,000 to $50,000 depending on your vehicle's current market value and any existing balance owing. Call (780) 995-1579 for an instant estimate — no paperwork required for the initial quote.
Can I keep driving my car during the loan?
Yes. You keep driving your vehicle throughout the entire loan term. Fast Cash Auto holds the title as collateral — your car never leaves your possession.
What are the rates and terms?
APR ranges from 9%–35% depending on vehicle value and loan term. Terms up to 3 years. No early payout penalty. Example: $5,600 over 12 months at 31.76% APR = $7,661.92 total. Full terms disclosed before you sign anything.
