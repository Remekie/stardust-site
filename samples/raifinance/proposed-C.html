Common questions

How car title loans work.

How accurate is the estimate?
The estimator uses vehicle age and mileage to produce a reasonable range based on typical equity values in Alberta. The actual confirmed amount may be higher or lower depending on the vehicle's current condition and market value. The real number is confirmed on the call — no hard inquiry, no obligation.
How do car title loans work?
A car title loan uses your vehicle as collateral. You keep driving while the loan is outstanding. We hold the title as security — not the car. Approval is based on vehicle equity. No credit check required.
Do I need good credit to qualify?
No. We do not check credit. We approve applicants with bad credit, no credit, previous bankruptcy, consumer proposals, and new immigrants with no Canadian credit file.
Can I keep my car during the loan?
Yes. You keep driving throughout the entire loan term. We hold the title as collateral only — the car stays with you.
